Curiosity Drop

Discover, Scan, and Win!

Curiosity Drop is a fun, local experience designed to engage your customers while helping your business gain exposure. Customers scan our small QR posters to unlock rewards, surprises, or exclusive content — and every scan is a win!

For Businesses Hosting a Drop

If you display a Curiosity Drop poster at your location, you’ll:

  • Drive foot traffic and engagement in your store.
  • Encourage repeat visits and word-of-mouth buzz.
  • Get listed on our Drop Locations map for extra community exposure.
  • Show your customers that your business is innovative and fun — all at no cost.

For Businesses Joining the Spin2Win Rotation

If you’d like your business to appear as a reward in our Spin2Win rotation, you’ll:

  • Be featured on the landing page as a prize customers can unlock — must include a discount, free item, or other benefit for your customers.
  • Drive new customers to your location through the reward system.
  • Receive exposure in a playful, memorable way that builds brand awareness.
  • Participate in a low-cost, high-engagement marketing program designed to connect with curious local shoppers.
