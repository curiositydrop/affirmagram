Discover, Scan, and Win!

Curiosity Drop is a fun, local experience designed to engage your customers while helping your business gain exposure. Customers scan our small QR posters to unlock rewards, surprises, or exclusive content — and every scan is a win!

How it Works

  • Place a small QR poster in your store or near the exit.
  • Customers scan it and are taken to a playful landing page.
  • Every scan generates engagement, excitement, and foot traffic.

Benefits for Your Business

  • Get listed on our Drop Locations map for extra exposure.
  • Encourage repeat visits and word-of-mouth buzz.
  • Show your community that you’re innovative and fun — all at no cost.
