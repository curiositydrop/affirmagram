About BANDtroductions

BANDtroductions is a platform created to help bands and musicians connect through real performance videos instead of just text profiles.

Musicians can upload their performances, showcase their talent, and connect with bands searching for new members. Bands can introduce themselves, share their music, and discover musicians that fit their sound and style.

The goal is to create a space where talent can be discovered organically, whether it's a local band searching for their next member or a rising musician being discovered by the wider music community.

As the platform grows, BANDtroductions aims to become a hub for discovering emerging artists, helping connect musicians, bands, and eventually even industry professionals looking for the next great sound.
