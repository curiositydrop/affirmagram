About School Street Coffeehouse

The School Street Coffeehouse is a welcoming community music venue hosted by the Unitarian Universalist Church of Saco and Biddeford in Saco. Our coffeehouse brings together music lovers, local performers, and touring artists in an intimate listening room dedicated to live acoustic music and meaningful connection. With excellent sound, warm hospitality, and coffee, tea, and homemade desserts, School Street Coffeehouse creates a relaxed space where audiences can discover new voices, support working musicians, and share in the simple joy of live performance.
