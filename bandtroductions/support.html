Why BANDtroductions Exists

The local music scene is full of incredible talent — but it isn’t always easy for bands, musicians, venues, and fans to find each other.

BANDtroductions was created to solve that by building a central hub where:

  • 🎸 Bands can get discovered
  • 🥁 Musicians can find bands
  • 🎤 Venues can easily discover local talent
  • 🎶 Fans can explore and support local music

This platform is completely free for bands, musicians, and venues to join.
