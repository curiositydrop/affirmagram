About Bootleg

Videos and photos to follow soon! The three of us were in a band together for several years. We left the band and are starting fresh with the 3 of us (nothing bad about the previous band, we just had different ideas of time commitments, etc.) We choose 2-3 songs to practice on our own in between practices, pulling them together when we meet. We're aiming to get together once/month for either practice or gigs in summer months, occasionally more, and during the winter months, a couple times/month or more). We all work and have busy lives but music is what keeps us sane. We're laid back, middle-aged group of folks. If you want to put a band together and are near the Blue Hill, Brooklin, Deer Isle areas, we'd love to hear from you! Hope to build something fun and pleasing to the ears by adding a lead guitarist and drummer (backing vocals or ability to handle some lead vocals are a huge plus!).
